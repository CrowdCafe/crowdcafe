Welcome to CrowdCafe

CrowdCafe - is a microtask marketplace, where people publish data classification tasks, and others - perform them for a reward.

Tasks

All tasks are grouped in 3 categories (Caffè, Cappuccino and Wine) with a fixed reward amount and a recommended execution time. The approximate wage on CrowdCafe is 10€/hour.

Caffè

0.03€ / 10 sec

Cappuccino

0.35€ / 2 min

Wine

1€ / 5 min

Rewards

The money you earn on CrowdCafe can be exchanged on rewards, such as free bus and train tickets, free coffee and deserts in local bars, movie and museum tickets, Bitcoins, Amazon credits and many other great things.

Quality control

There are real people performing real tasks with real data, published by real people paying real money. So there is no place for cheating or any inappropriate behavior on the platform. The quality is controlled by injecting in datasets ground truth data and several other ways.

CrowdCafe brings the best experience to tasks executors, so in case of not respectful behavior from the executors side we have to apply penalties and blocking accounts of violators.

Who are the people publishing tasks?

People who publish tasks on this platform are academic researchers, commercial companies and governmental organisations. They publish here tasks which are very hard to perform automatically using any algorithms, while humans can still perform them well. The results collected in these tasks have a great scientific and commercial impact.

Who has created CrowdCafe?

CrowdCafe was developed as an academic project at the University of Trento (Italy) by PhD students from the Social Informatics research group.
